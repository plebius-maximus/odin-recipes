Stew Recipe

Heart Vegan Stew:

This recipe will show you how to make a hearty vegan stew to enjoy during the winter months.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion minced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 large carrots sliced
  • 2 stalks celery sliced
  • 2 cups baby potatoes halved
  • 1 cup white mushrooms sliced & quartered
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 19 oz diced tomatoes canned with garlic
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 sprigs rosemary
  • small bunch fresh thyme
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 tbsp water

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil, then add onion and garlic; sauté 3 to 4 minutes or until onion is translucent.
  2. Add the carrots, celery, baby potatoes, mushrooms, and peas into the pot.
  3. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Add the Italian seasoning, vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, soy sauce, balsamic vinegar, rosemary, and thyme. Stir and bring to a boil.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water to form a slurry mix. Add to the stew and stir.
  6. Turn heat down, cover pot, and simmer for 45 minutes or until veggies are tender.
  7. Serve.
